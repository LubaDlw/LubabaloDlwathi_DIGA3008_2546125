DESIGN

OVERALL WEBSITE GOALS

The primary goal of this portfolio website is to create a professional digital presence that effectively showcases my skills, work, and thought process as a designer and developer. Beyond simply displaying projects, I aim to:

  • Demonstrate Technical Versatility – Show proficiency across multiple disciplines through both the content featured and the implementation of the site itself
  • Communicate Design Thinking – Reveal the rationale and process behind my work, not just the final outcomes
  • Create Meaningful Connections – Establish a platform that facilitates professional relationships with potential clients, employers, and collaborators
  • Balance Professionalism with Personality – Present my work with technical rigor while also conveying my unique perspective and approach
  • Prioritize Accessibility – Ensure the site is navigable and usable for visitors with diverse needs and browsing contexts

This website serves as both a showcase of my work and a demonstration of my ability to create user-centered digital experiences that balance functionality with aesthetic appeal.

WIREFRAMES

These initial wireframes represent the foundational structure for my portfolio site, focusing on creating a clear information hierarchy and intuitive user flow.

DESIGN RATIONALE

Navigation Philosophy

The consistent top navigation bar featuring clearly labeled pages keeps navigation visible at all times, prioritizing user clarity and reducing cognitive load.

Grid-Based Layout

Pages are built on a simple, responsive grid, ensuring content structure and alignment stays consistent across devices.

Content Hierarchy

  • Home Page – Starts with my identity and role, then displays highlights
  • Portfolio Page – Uses card-based structure and categories for filtering
  • Design Page – Combines visuals with explanation
  • Profile Page – Shows skills and bio in a clean vertical layout

White Space Utilization

  1. Improves readability
  2. Groups related content
  3. Conveys a refined aesthetic
  4. Reduces user fatigue

DESIGN INSPIRATIONS

Key Inspirations

  • Empathy Creative Studio – Minimal, code-aesthetic with soft animations
  • Collin Moy – Well-balanced layout, great use of whitespace

Reference Influences

Inspired by design giants like Apple, and industry leaders who prioritize clarity and spacing for legibility. I structured my own layouts around the actual content needs and flow, not rigid templates.
